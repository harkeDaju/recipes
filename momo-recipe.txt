Ingredients:
 - 1/2 pound buffalo minched meat
 - 1 chopped onion
 - 1/4 chopped cabbage
 - 4 chopped green onions
 - 5 cloves garlic smashed
 - 1 teaspoon of ginger
 - 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
 - 1 teaspoon of tumeric powder
 - 2 teaspoon of cumin powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper powder
 - 1 teaspoon of salt
 - dumpling wrappers

Directions:
 1. Mix all the ingredients together with hand.

 2. Put some meat in to the dumpling wrapper and wrap it closed.

 3. Put some water on the bottom pot of the dumping making pots. Bring water 
    to boil.

 4. Put the wrapped dumplings in the steamer pots. And put those pots on top
    of the boiling pots. The dumplings will get cooked by steam.
 
 5. Once the dumpling wrapper becomes shiny then they are ready to eat.
